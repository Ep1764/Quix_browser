About

Author: QuixLume
Description: What is Quix_Browser? Quix_Browser is a "Semi-Browser" using iframe to display the searched content!, developed by a 16 year old kid (QuixLume) who grew up having to deal with blocked games on a school computer, we all have a common enemy; who is this enemy? well you might know GoGuardian, a 3rd party extension used by most school districs to prevent kids from doing anything non school related on a enterprised enrolled device.What does Quix_Browser offer? Quix_Browser offers multiple security protection; this includes the "Change Tab Title" feature that allows you to rename the tab title, with this feature a teacher might not recongnize what the tab really is meaning the teacher won't close your tab using GoGuardian!. Note that the dis-advantage of using iframe is that some websites don't allow iframe thus preventing you from accessing them; here's a few websites that don't allow iframe: Youtube, Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, Tiktokand much more, as sad as it seems Quix_Browser promises to garenteed access to blocked ".io" games!!!
